Interactive Self-Learning Robot

A Glimpse into Machine Learning This fascinating project provides an interactive window into the world of artificial intelligence, specifically reinforcement learning. You are presented with a simple car (our “robot”) on a track. Its goal? To learn how to navigate the track on its own. By clicking “Train”, you can watch as the robot begins its learning process through trial and error. It explores the environment, occasionally making mistakes, but gradually building a “brain” or a policy that guides it toward successful navigation.
